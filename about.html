About Us

Welcome to Vila Malo 2 Nestled in the heart of Ksamil, Vila Malo 2 offers a serene retreat just an 8-minute stroll from the pristine Ksamil Beach 9 and a short distance from the picturesque Paradise Beach. Our charming accommodations provide the perfect blend of comfort and convenience, featuring a private entrance for your ease.

Our air-conditioned apartments are thoughtfully designed with your comfort in mind. Each unit boasts a private terrace with stunning pool views, a cozy seating area, and a satellite flat-screen TV. You'll find a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge, as well as a private bathroom with a bidet and hairdryer. Step out onto your balcony to enjoy an outdoor dining area with breathtaking city views. We provide all the essentials, including bed linen and towels, to ensure a restful stay.

Join us at Vila Malo 2 for an unforgettable stay in Ksamil, where comfort meets convenience in a beautiful coastal setting. Whether you're here for a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a solo adventure, we look forward to welcoming you to your home away from home.
