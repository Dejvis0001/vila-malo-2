Who We Are ?

Vila Malo 2

Discover Vila Malo 2, ideally located in Ksamil, just 700 meters from Ksamil Beach 9. Enjoy stunning sea views, free WiFi, and parking. Our hotel offers family rooms, BBQ facilities, and air-conditioned units with private bathrooms, flat-screen TVs, and balconies. Relax in our garden or indoor play area, and explore nearby cycling routes.

Lori Beach is under 1 km away, and Butrint National Park is a short 2.6 km, providing easy access to the region's natural beauty and history.
